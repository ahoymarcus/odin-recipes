Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.

  2. Step 2

    3. In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder. Spread batter into prepared pan.

  3. Step 3

    4. Bake in preheated oven for 25 to 30 minutes. Do not overcook.

  4. Step 4

    5. Remove brownies from the oven, and make frosting. Combine 3 tablespoons softened butter, 3 tablespoons cocoa, honey, 1 teaspoon vanilla extract, and 1 cup confectioners' sugar. Stir until smooth.

  5. Step 5

    6. Frost brownies while they are still warm.
